Game Instructions

  • Chose your username, this must not be empty and needs at least 5 characters
  • Hit the play button
  • A message will appear, when ready, hit start for starting the game
  • You will have 10 multiple answers questions
  • Chose an aswer by clicking on it
  • The chosen answer will turn green if correct and red if incorrect
  • If you chosen the incorrect answer, the correct answer will also be displayed
  • After answering all 10 questions, your final score will be displayed
  • If you would like to play again, just hit the play again button, and 10 new questions will be loaded.
